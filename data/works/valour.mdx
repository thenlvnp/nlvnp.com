### Background

This is the University of the Immaculate Conception 2019 Official Yearbook. the theme revolves around greek roman, most of the assets found in this project are 3D modeled and finished and manipulated in Adobe Photoshop.

### The Logo

One of the most significant asset in this project is the logo of the Immaculatus Publication which was adapted to match the theme. The marbled Immaculatus logo that has gold inner material and is treated as cracked and vintage marble. It helped shape the concept and the assets around it.
